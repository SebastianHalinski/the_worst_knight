Hot Cold Warm game ! 

Program random 3-digit number with no repeating digits, 
and you try to guess what the number is. 
After each guess, program gives you three types of clues:

Cold – None of the three digits you guessed 
       is in the secret number.

Warm – One of the digits is in the secret number, 
       but your guess has the digit in the wrong place.

Hot – Your guess has a correct digit 
      in the correct place.

You have 10 guesses.
